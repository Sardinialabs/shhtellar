Privacy Policy

Shhtellar — Last updated: March 26, 2026

Sardinia Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Shhtellar mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Anonymous Authentication

When you first open the App, we automatically create an anonymous account using Firebase Authentication. This generates a unique identifier (UID) that is not linked to your personal identity. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Gameplay Data

When you participate in the Daily Challenge mode, we collect:

  • Your anonymous user ID
  • Your completion time
  • Your score
  • The date of the challenge

This data is used solely to provide the leaderboard functionality and to prevent replaying the same daily challenge.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, IDFA)
  • Photos, contacts, or other device data
  • Payment or financial information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to:

  • Display the daily challenge leaderboard
  • Track daily challenge completion status
  • Improve the gameplay experience

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties for any purpose.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — for anonymous user identification. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) — for storing gameplay scores. Firebase Privacy Policy

These services may collect limited technical data (such as IP address) as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

Gameplay data is retained for as long as the leaderboard is active. Anonymous authentication data is retained until the App is uninstalled or the user's data is cleared.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App only uses anonymous authentication and does not collect any personal data, it is suitable for users of all ages.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Firebase to protect your data. All data transmission between the App and our servers is encrypted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
